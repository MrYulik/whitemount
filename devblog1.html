    New models, inventory system, shooting system , new network core, and more.

    MrYulik

    Part of me was building the weapons system, and moving the project to the new core of the network, little by little.
    Let's start with the fact that the first firearm is called gcock-10:
    - Its speed is 0.5
    - firing range: 400 meters
    - damage: 25 to the body, 75 to the head
    - the maximum number of rounds in the magazine 12
    - cartridge type: pistol bullet
    - There is recoil and spread of bullets.
    There are also bullet holes.
    My next task was to create an IR character to rotate the back of the bone and do other visual effects.
    And I had a big task to transfer the project to a new network core, the reason for the transition: the old system was not very stable,
    there was also a ping on the server.
    All systems such as character movement, weapon system, player UI, player IK system have been synchronized,
    tests were carried out and a large number of bugs were found, but today I have already fixed them. And as one person from the team said.
    "Fun to play"
    That's all, if in detail, then it will not be interesting to read, I talked about the most global systems that appeared in the game.
